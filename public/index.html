Goalkeeping, as in Soccer!

I was raised in a country that loves baseball but somehow I got attached to a small sport named soccer (I'd found out later that it is actually not that small of a sport). I started playing when I was 6 years old and although I was never technically gifted my size at that time helped me overcome those early steps. When I turned 8 I started Goalkeeping by chance, and never looked back. Nowadays, my favorite thing to do is go out and play.

The things I like the most about soccer are:

  • It is simple
  • It is very educating
  • It is fun
  • It brings people together
